  • PTO Power Supply: Attached to tractor using 3-point linkage and powered via PTO (540/1000 RPM).
  • Gear Transmission: PTO drives gearbox, transferring power to rotary shaft.
  • Blade Rotation: Rotary shaft rotates blades at 200â€“300 RPM.
  • Soil Pulverization: Blades cut, lift, and turn soil; clods are crushed.
  • Residue Mixing & Leveling: Fertilizer/residues mix evenly; soil is flattened by leveling board.
