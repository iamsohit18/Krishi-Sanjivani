Role of Technology in Agriculture

Technology has become the backbone of modern agriculture. It helps farmers access smart solutions for farming, market connectivity, financial services, and sustainable growth. The services provided on this platform are powered by technology to meet every agricultural need effectively.

Benefits of Technology in Agriculture

Technology in agriculture is not just about machines or apps â€” it's about empowering farmers with knowledge, market access, and modern practices that increase productivity, reduce wastage, and ensure a sustainable future for farming.

Smart Farming Technologies Used Today:

  • IoT Sensors for soil & water monitoring
  • Drones for crop spraying & land mapping
  • AI & Machine Learning for pest control & smart advisory
  • Blockchain for secure supply chain management
  • Mobile Apps for market prices & agri-updates
  • GIS & GPS for precision farming
